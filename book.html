Overview

Rendezvous Hotel Singapore is located in the Arts and Heritage District, within 3 km (0.2 mi) of the Singapore National Museum, Singapore Art Museum, and Singapore Management University. The Dhoby Ghaut MRT (Mass Rapid Transit) station is a five-minute walk, and Orchard Road shops are a 10-to-15-minute walk. Hotel Features. The exterior of the building combines history and sophistication, while the interior offers an art-infused experience and a unique aesthetic. The hotel offers an outdoor pool, and a fitness center. Public areas and all guestrooms are nonsmoking. Straits [email protected] offers buffet and a la carte dining. Room service is available around the clock. A lobby bar and a gourmet food court are also on site. The hotel offers a concierge desk, a multilingual staff, and business services. Guestrooms. The air-conditioned guestrooms feature cable TV, pay movies, and in-room safes. Wireless and wired high-speed Internet access are available for a surcharge. Minibars and coffee/tea makers are also included. Bathrooms offer shower/tub combinations, handheld showers, and telephones. Wake-up calls are provided upon request
